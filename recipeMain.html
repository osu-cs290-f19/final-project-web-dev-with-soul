A Very Nice Bowl of Top Ramen
Cook Time: 120 minutes Rating: 5
  • Top Ramen (1 packet)
  • Water (3 cups)
  1. Heat up the water in a pot on the stove until boiling
  2. Insert the noodles from the top ramen packet into the boiling water and wait 3 minutes
  3. Open up the flavor packet and dump contents into pot with boiling noodes. Stir.
  4. Once fully mixed, take pot off the heat and pour the ramen and broth into a serving bowl.
  5. Enjoy!
